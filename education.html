Education

Degrees, certifications, awards, and acknowledgements over my academic career

I was born and raised in rural New Brunswick. From a very young age, I had a keen interest in space, science, and mathematics. In high school, I was highly involved with sports, clubs, and music. I participated in leadership activities and excelled in academics. I graduated as valedictorian of my class, and was eager to pursue my education in university. I moved to Halifax to study astrophysics at Saint Mary’s University. I graduated with Honours in 2003 and moved to Hawaii to pursue a Masters degree in physics. While working toward my masters degree at the University of Hawaii at Manoa, I lived and worked in Antarctica for 3 months, worked on experiments at the Stanford, hiked volcanoes, and learned to surf.
