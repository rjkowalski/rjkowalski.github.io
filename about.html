About Me

A short history of my academic and professional experience

I am a data engineer and technical project manager with more than 15 years experience in technology research, development, and innovation involving the private sector, government, research laboratories, and educational institutions. I am a self-motivated individual with strong leadership skills, interpersonal skills, and organizational abilities that excels at research and development, systems integration, data analysis, and scientific problem solving. I'm highly adaptable, flexible, and curious to learn and face new challenges.

Currently, I am data engineer with Nova Scotia Health focused on developing data pipelines for machine learning projects in diagnostic imaging (MRI, PET, CT, Ultrasound). Before starting this position, I was the technical project manager for the IWK Health Centre where I managed an ~8 Million dollar project developing a suite of software tools for a 3T MRI system within Atlantic Canada's largest research hospital.

Prior to starting in the public sector, I was the CTO for a dental technology company where I led a team of software developers in producing a cloud-based (AWS) SaaS product providing services to customers in North/South America and Europe. I gained further experience in the private sector working with a multi-national company developing Python-based test automation software for products delivered to millions of customers.

I have 8 years of scientific research experience as a physicist/astorphysicist during which I have been involved in the flight operations of two independent NASA Long Duration Balloon (LDB) flights in New Mexico and Antarctica. I have a strong background in data analysis, systems integration, software development, and project management.
